CRICKET FAN PAGE

Welcome to the ultimate cricket fan zone!

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players each. The game is played on a circular or oval-shaped field with a 22-yard pitch at the center. Each team takes turns to bat and bowl, with the aim of scoring the most runs.

Sir Donald Bradman

                
Full Name: Sir Donald George Bradman
Born: August 27, 1908, in Cootamundra, New South Wales, Australia
Died: February 25, 2001, in Kensington Park, Adelaide, South Australia
Nicknames: The Don
Batting Style: Right-hand batz
Career Stats:
- Matches: 52
- Runs: 6,996
- Average: 99.94
Achievements:
- Highest Test batting average of 99.94, a record that remains unmatched.

Sachin Tendulkar

                
Full Name: Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar
Born: April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Nicknames: Little Master
Batting Style: Right-hand bat
Career Stats:
- Matches: 200
- Runs: 15,921
- Centuries: 51
Achievements:
- Only player to score 100 international centuries.
- Most runs in international cricket.

Shane Warne

                
Full Name: Shane Keith Warne
Born: September 13, 1969, Ferntree Gully, Victoria, Australia
Role: Bowler
Bowling Style: Right-arm leg break
Career Stats:
- Matches: 145
- Wickets: 708
Achievements:
- First bowler to take 700 Test wickets.
- Delivered the 'Ball of the Century' in 1993.

Virat Kohli

                
Full Name: Virat Kohli
Born: November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India
Nicknames: King Kohli, Chase Master
Batting Style: Right-hand bat
Career Stats:
- Matches: 111 (as of 2023)
- Runs: 8,500+
- Centuries: 28 Test centuries, 46 ODI centuries
Achievements:
- Fastest to reach 10,000 runs in ODI cricket.
- Leading run-scorer for India in international cricket, surpassing multiple records.

Brian Lara

                
Full Name: Brian Charles Lara
Born: May 2, 1969, Santa Cruz, Trinidad and Tobago
Nicknames: The Prince of Port of Spain
Batting Style: Left-hand bat
Career Stats:
- Matches: 131
- Runs: 11,953
- Highest Score: 400* (the highest individual score in Test cricket)
Achievements:
- Holds the record for the highest individual score in both Test (400*) and first-class cricket (501*).

Muttiah Muralitharan

                
Full Name: Muttiah Muralitharan
Born: April 17, 1972, Kandy, Sri Lanka
Role: Bowler
Bowling Style: Right-arm off-break
Career Stats:
- Matches: 133
- Wickets: 800
Achievements:
- The highest wicket-taker in Test cricket with 800 wickets.
